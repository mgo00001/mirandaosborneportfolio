Original Typeface: Frankenstein

  • Medium: Typeface, Poster
  • Date: Fall 2022
  • Programs Used: FontStruct

This is an original typeface that was created and inspired by the malformed figure of the monster from Mary Shelly's Frankenstien. Letterforms are mishapen and uneven to contribute to the feeling of chaos the novel exudes.

A common trend in this typeface is to have top heavy forms with a thick, bold presence, while the bottom feels like it could crumble from the weight at any moment. To in a way advertise this type, a freakout showcasing the type in action was created.
