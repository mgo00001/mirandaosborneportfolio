Dangers of Youtube Collage

  • Medium: Digital Collage
  • Date: Fall 2022
  • Programs Used: Adobe Photoshop

This digital collage was inspired by the TEDTalk "The Nightmare of Children's Youtube" by James Bridle. In this TEDTalk, Bridle goes on to descirbe how automation and the algotrithim of the video sharing platform can be harmful and even dangerous to young children. When creating this piece, one of the main themes it aimed to accomplish was the feeling of danger and dread caused by unsettling videos aimed for children.

As a great chance to test photography and collaboration skills, a fellow peer's arm and my own personal cables were used to create the scene in the final execution. As with other projects, this process began with various sketches of how to depict this danger of YouTube. Moving on from sketches involved creating mockups to begin truly visualizing the concept. It is both exciting and satisfying to see the final image begin to come together. Overall, this project assisted in improving image editing expertise as well as experimenting with coneying visual metaphors.
